Dear Fictional, Your Affection
(DF, YA)
is an experimental interactive web project that delves into
the phenomenon of
parasocial relationships
with fictional characters,
particularly under the influence of
new media
in modern society.
MORE
This project consists of an essay and 11 interviews, with all content distributed across four distinct sections in a narrative sequence. Please be aware that some interview excerpts include personal subjective perspectives. The primary aim is to share diverse viewpoints and ideas rather than to engage in critique. As you read, please keep this in mind, and let this virtual world be filled with love and respect for others. If you are ready, let us begin!
